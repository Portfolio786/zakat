📌 Fitrana Information

  • Fitrana is Zakat al-Fitr, payable before Eid-ul-Fitr prayers
  • Standard rate: 1.6 kg of wheat or rice per person (or equivalent in cash)
  • It is obligatory for all Muslims including children
  • Can be paid in cash or kind (wheat/rice)
  • Must be paid before Eid prayers
  • Given to the poor and needy
