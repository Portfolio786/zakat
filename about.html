About Islamic Tools Hub

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Islamic Tools Hub is dedicated to providing free, accurate, and easy-to-use Islamic calculation tools for Muslims worldwide. Our mission is to help Muslims fulfill their religious obligations with confidence and accuracy.

What We Offer

We provide a comprehensive suite of Islamic tools including:

  • Zakat Calculator - Calculate your obligatory Zakat (2.5% of wealth)
  • Fitrana Calculator - Calculate Fitrana for Eid al-Fitr
  • Prayer Times - Get accurate prayer times for your location
  • Hijri Calendar - Convert between Gregorian and Islamic dates
  • Dua Generator - Access daily Islamic prayers and duas

  • ✓ Mobile-Friendly - Works on all devices

Our Team

Islamic Tools Hub is built by a team of developers passionate about helping Muslims practice their faith with ease and confidence. We believe that technology should serve humanity and help people connect with their religion.

Accuracy & Sources

All our calculations are based on authentic Islamic principles and methods recognized by scholars worldwide. We use verified APIs for prayer times and date conversions to ensure accuracy.
