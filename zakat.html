📋 Zakat Guidelines

  • Zakat is obligatory on Muslims who meet the nisab threshold
  • Nisab is the minimum wealth required to pay Zakat
  • Zakat rate is 2.5% of total wealth
  • It is one of the Five Pillars of Islam
  • Zakat is usually paid during Ramadan, especially before Eid
  • Ensure your calculations are accurate and paid on time
